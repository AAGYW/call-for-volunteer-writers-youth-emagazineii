About the Magazine

Cameroon has a vibrant generation of young people who are innovating, leading, and reshaping society across multiple domains. Yet, many of their stories remain under-reported or invisible in mainstream media. The Cameroon Youth E‑Magazine will be a digital platform created by the Association of Adolescent Girls and Young Women with and for young people to profile, celebrate, and amplify youth achievements in diverse sectors.

This magazine will not just inform but inspire. It will become a go‑to digital space where young people see themselves reflected, their voices valued, and their achievements celebrated. Here’s your chance to join a bold new platform that celebrates, amplifies, and connects the achievements of young people in Cameroon.

Who We’re Looking For

  • Interested in youth issues and achievements,

What You’ll Gain

  • The chance to get your work published and read widely across Cameroon.
  • Build writing and digital media skills with mentorship and peer support.
  • Grow your network with other young changemakers and professionals.
  • Be part of a pioneering youth‑led movement inspiring the next generation.

How to Apply

  2. Short writing sample (article, essay, blog post, or creative piece — max 500 words).
